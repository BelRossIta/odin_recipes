King Crap Appitizers

Description

King Crab Appetizers are a savory favorite for seafood lovers around the world! The tartlet have delicious taste and a satisfying crunch!

Ingredients

  • 2(12 ounce) packages of refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 1(8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1(6 ounce) can crab meat, drained
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoon of thinly sliced green onion
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch of paprika

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat oven to 375 degree F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease 12 tarlet pans.

  2. Step 2

    Divide the rolls in half and press them into the prepared tartlet pans, then set aside

  3. Step 3

    In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, crab, mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, cheddar cheese, green onionss and Worcestershire sauce. Spoon 1 teaspoon of mixture into tarts and garnish with paprika.

  4. Step 4

    Back at 375 degrees F(190 degrees C) for 15 to 20 minutes, or until brown. These freeze wonderfully. Just reheat before serving.
