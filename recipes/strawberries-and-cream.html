Aqua Fresca de Fresas con Creama (Strawberries and Cream)

Description

This delicious creamy strawberry aqua fresca will have your taste buds thinking you're eating dessert!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds of strawberries, hulled and diced, divided
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 (14 1/2 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup of sugar, or as needed
  • 3 cups of ice cubes, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Place 2 pounds of the strawberries and water in a blender cup and blend until smooth.

  2. Step 2

    Pour strawberry puree into a large pitcher. Add sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Stir until well incorporated. Taste; add sugar, a little at a time, as needed

  3. Step 3

    Add ice cubes and stir. Gently stir in remaining 1/2 pounds of diced strawberries. Chill until ready to serve.
